Valir

Damage/Guard

Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Newcomer of Academy

Task Target: Deal 20,000 damage to enemy heroes in total with Burst Fire.

Legend says there is a beautiful and fertile country at the edge of the western barrens. That is where Valir comes from. When Valir first got to the Academy of Magic, he displayed extraordinary talent and a mastery of arcane magic. The teacher of the arcane arts class, Gord, was even reminded of his younger self upon seeing Valir. But Valir was completely obsessed with arcane fire. He invited new spells constantly, until he finally mastered Burst Fire. This made Valir unbeatable in battle.

Chapter 2 : Fierce Flame

Task Target: Deal 15,000 damage to enemy heroes in total with Ashing.

Gord began to wholeheartedly teach this young outlander all that he had learned about the control of arcane energy. But the energy surrounding Valir began to make Gord uneasy. It was an energy even more violent than arcane energy. It turned out that Valir had combined the flame spells from his homeland with arcane power to create a unique form of arcane flame magic. One time during training, Valir accidentally cast Searing Torrent, an exceptionally violent flame spell, which enraged his teacher Gord.

Chapter 3 : Duel with Mentor

Task Target: Perform 1 Triple kills with an Ultimate active.

Gord felt that Valir had completely betrayed him as his teacher, tarnishing his pure arcane energy with wild fire. Furious, Gord tried to use pure arcane energy to remove the arcane flame magic from within Valir's body. Valir wielded his Arcane Flame against his teacher and even cast Hellfire, a spell so powerful and violent that it destroyed half of the Academy of Magic.

Chapter 4 : Escape

Task Target: Win 2 matches against Heroes from the Magic Academy.

The proud Gord was shocked to discover that with wild Arcane Flame, his disciple could hold his own against Gord's pure arcane magic. In the end Valir ran away from the academy, and Gord resigned from his position to search for his lost disciple in his arcane flying machine. Additionally, Valir had become the target of pursuit for both the Eruditio City Guard and the Academy of Magic. He often has to face challenges from his peers in the academy.

Story

In the southwest of the Land of Dawn lies an active volcano that brought deadly peril to all who dared approach. "Inferno of Fire", was what people called this place of trial for Mages eager to master flame spells. After leaving the magic academy, Valir dwelled himself there for five years while carrying a ten - year promise with Vale. He hoped that day would come more quickly, but at the same time , he also hoped that day would never come. One day, Valir cast his arcane flame magic towords the volcano but the result was not to his expectation.

In the western desert, there was a man who was looking for Valir. The betrayal of years was something he could never forgive. With great anger and hate, and to maintain the reputation of the magic academy, he decided to track down the traitor. The moment he felt an unusual arcane power releasing from the volcano, he smile.

The long-term inhabitance in this "Inferno of Fire" brought changes to Valir body. His skin was burned by the flames of the volcano , but in order to become the Son of Flames, he had to combine the power of his arcane flame magic with the volcano's in inferno fire. His body and his will must willstand the trials of there scalding lava. Suddenly, a strong wind blew, bringing a familiar sense to Valir. Vale had come! They had never met each other since childhood. This encounter led to the first fight between them, between fire and wind. The Windtalker and the Son of Frames cast their all magic to attack each other. No words were exchanged, but they shared a common thought - they didn't want to kill each other. However, their fight generated great power, which was aggravated the volcano. If the volcano burst, they would surely die in an inferno.

Five years left - can't you wait Vale!?

Before Vale Vale said anything, Valir heard another familiar voice, it was Gord. Gord's presence interrupted this fight between these brothers. He wanted to abolish the arcane magic of Valir, because in his mind, his pure arcane wisdom should never be tarnished. However, the arcane spell he cast to Valir was stopped by Vale Vale. Vale Vale turned to sympathy upon looking at Valir's tortured body. He didn't want anyone to hurt his brother. However, Valir didn't need such sympathy.

One of them was his master who wanted to destroy his apprentice for his apparent betrayal.

Another one of them had been his closest brother. However, he was also the son of the one who killed Valir's father.

The sacred flame would never be quenched. Valir successfully combined the power of his arcane flame magic with the inferno fire and burst out a powerful energy. With this power, the lava became unstable. Meanwhile, Valir gained a mighty body while his eyes became scarlet flames. At that time, Vale could understand his father's worries. This kind of power astonished Gord, and as his teacher, he would rather kill his student than to let him be possessed by this crazy power.

Valir's flame, Vale's wind, and's arcane power aggravated the volcano, which led to a great collapse. After that, Vale and Gord tried to find Valir, but they couldn't find him. Vale knew that in his heart Valir was still alive, and that their ten-year promise was not yet broken. The five-years made Valir formidable, what was to happen after another five years? Vale knew the next time they encountered each other, it would no longer be as friends and brothers. Rather, it would be about the greatest fight for their lives.

Ashing

CC
Damage
Passive

Valir's skills ignite his targets ablaze, every second dealing Magic Damage equal to 0.6% of their Max HP. Lasts for 4s.

When blaze stacks 3 times on a target, it causes an explosion, dealing Magic Damage equal to 6% of target's Max HP and stunning the target for 1s. (Within 7s, the same target can only be stunned by Valir's skills once.)

Burst Fireball

AoE
Slowed
CD: 1.5 Mana Cost: 50 Type: Magic Skill

Valir casts a fireball in a designated direction. The fireball will explode upon hitting an enemy, dealing 235 (+80% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies in the explosion area and slowing them by 30% for 1s. Also ignite the ground below and cause another explosion after a short delay, dealing 118(+40% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to enemies in the area.

Valir can store up to 2 Burst Fireball charges and will gain a charge if the skill hits an enemy hero first or kills a minion or creep.

Searing Torrent

CC
AoE
CD: 13.0 Mana Cost: 80 Type: Magic Skill

Valir unleashes a torrent of flames forward, knocking enemies back to the very end of its range and dealing 200( +60% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to them.

After being enhanced by the Vengeance Flame, the Searing Torrent will generate a firewall at the very end of its range that lasts for 4s, dealing 80 (+24% Total Magic Power) Magic Damage to nearby enemies every second.

The torrent of flames and firewall can slow enemies by 25% for 1s.
