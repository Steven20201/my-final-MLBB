Mobile Legends: Project Next | First Phase

5th Anniversary Celebration Event

All Revemped Heroes in First Phase

Mobile Legends was turning five years in October 2021. Players can access to Project NEXT is Mobile Legends: Bang Bang's biggest changes, and it plans to improve heros balance by revamping skills, redesigning characters models, game optimization and more.

In project NEXT updates, they divided to three phrases. The first one was relasing on 22nd September 2020 and the second phase was relasing by end of 2020 There are a lot of changes in this updates, therefore here is a short summary of the changes for the old 6 heroes.

Not only their appearance changed but also their skills and their entrance animation also improved and they are a best meta hero and stronger than the previous time.

Introduction video about Project NEXT
Summary Changes for those heroes
Miya
❮ ❯
  • Turbo(Passive) - Each time Miya hits a target with basic attack, the attack speed will increase and the number of stacks is now shown above the miya's head. After reaching 5 stacks, she can summons Moonlight shadow that has massive physical damage.
  • Rain of Arrows(Skill 2) - has extra effect, when Mira shoots, the arrow will split into 8 minor arrows at the different location and the target will be immobilized.
  • Turbo Stealth(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - remove all stuns that enemies forcus on her and immediately gain full stacks of the Turbo effect to increase Movement.
Miya revamp
Saber
❮ ❯
  • Flying Sword(Skill 1) - Numbers of flying swords increased from 4 to 5. Those swords will attack the target and disappear when saber performs basic attack.
  • Charge(Skill 2) - has an ability to slow enemy as soon as saber use basic attak to that enemy.
  • Twiple Sweep(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - The skill's final blow will now double amount of damage.
Saber revamp
Eudora
❮ ❯
  • Superconductor(Passive) - the effects will increase when Eudora uses skills to enemy if targets are affected by Superconductor.
  • Forked Lightning(Skill 1) - Extra damage is given to all targets.
  • Electric Arrow(Skill 2) - It is a major upgrade to her old one as her second skill allows to stun multiple enemies at once.
  • Thunder's Wrath(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - Enhances the destructive ability of this skill more significantly.
Eudora revamp
Zilong
❮ ❯
  • Dragon Flurry(Passive) - After every 3 basic attacks, Zilong will gain his passive on his Basic Attack.
  • Spear Flip(Skill 1) - Zilong lifts an enemy over his back.
  • Spear Strike(Skill 2) - It has extra effect, when Zilong kills an enemy, there will be no cooldown and reset the kill.
  • Destruction Rush(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - Gain 40% Movement speed, increase attack speed and slow immunity for 7.5 second.
Zilong revamp
Layla
❮ ❯
  • Malefic Gun(Passive) - Gain Extra damage when she hits the further target
  • Malefic Bomb(Skill 1) - It has a new features, if she hits enemy, she will gain extra 60% additional Movement Speed that decays fast
  • Void Projectile(Skill 2) - It has extra effect, when she fires an energy sphere, it will leave the Magic mark on the eneny that will make enemies slowed by 30%.
  • Destruction Rush(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - Fires an energy blast, dealing with 37% of damage. Each upgrade of her ult level also increases second skill range and basic attack range.
Layla revamp
Alucard
❮ ❯
  • Pursuit(Passive) - Remove the extra 10% additional Physical Damage and enhance the damage from 20% to 50%.
  • Fission Wave:(Skill 3 or Ultimate) - It can mark all the targets within the rage instead of marking a single target and 50 % more passive damage to all the targets that have the mark.
Alucard revamp
