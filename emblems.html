Mobile Legends Emblems

Everything you need to know about Emblems in Mobile Legends

Information about Emblems

In Mobile Legends, the emblem is one of the most important things you should know. The level itself starts from level 1 until it reaches its maximum level, which is level 60 which you can increase by using magic dust and battle points.

How many emblems are there in this Mobile Legends? The answer is there are 9 sets of emblems. Two of them (Physical emblem and Magical emblem) are general emblem sets that have been opened automatically from the beginning. The rest of 7 emblem sets are unlocked when your account has reached level 10.

There are 9 emblem sets in Mobile Legends. They are Physical Emblem, Magical Emblem, Tank Emblem,Jungle Emblem, Assassin Emblem, Mage Emblem, Fighter Emblem, Support Emblem and Marksman Emblem.

Physical Emblem

If you are below level 10, u can only use Physical and Magical Emblems. This emblem is quite universe and can use by many heroes. Mostly, you can use for fighter or playing as a physical hero more often.

This emblem has several talents that include vitality, Firmness, Shield, Bravery, Swift, and Fatal.

For the choice of hero emblem, this is actually quite universal. As long as you use a physical hero that requres balance and damage, this emblem can be used consistently.

Magical Emblem

This Magical Emblem is one of the sets that can get free from the beginning and can hlep players who want to use mage-type of heroes

There are 6 talents, including Energy, Vitality, Awaken, Flow, Desire, and also Observation and the main talents are Energy Absorption and Magic Power Surge.

This Emblem can provide additional stats of magic power, HP Regen, Cooldown Reduction, HP and Mana. Although this emblem is not as good as mage emblem, it is quite a useful emblem set for the beginners.

Tank Emblem

In its use, it usually uses a tank set emblem, usually it can add Physical Defense, HP, Magical Defense, CD Reduction, and HR Regen stats.

Although this ML Emblem is less popular to be improved because outside the tank it is not so good, but you must maximize this emblem. Very strong with the tank advantage of this emblem, you can even use it in some fighters such as chou, martis who like to play as a tank.

For this Tank, there are 6 talents, including Vitality, Firmness, Shield, Inspire, Fourtress, and also Purity.

Jungle Emblem

For the players who like jungle, this emblem set is perfect for them to use. Because with the emblem set, it can be easily for players when they killed the jungle monsters.

This Jungle set also has 6 talent sets including Thrill, Brutal, Balance, Swift, Knowledge and Iron. Moreover, the main talents are Veteran Hunter, Wild Power and Mage Killer.

This emblem is not as popular as assassin emblem but due to the current meta, it is used by some players.

Assassin Emblem

The emblem set is very suitable for not only assassin heroes but also Marksman or Fighter heroes because it can increase the physical Penetration stat, Physical attack, Critical Chance, CD and movement Speed.

They also have talents that you cannot underestimate because they can make your heroes have high damage. Mandatory is usually increased.

This emblem is very powerful thanks to the stats boosted and it is the emblem with the highest impact.

Mage Emblem

This Mage Emblem is more specific than the regular Magical Emblem because it can add Magical Penetration which is an attribute that increase the effectiveness of magic damage by ignoring the magic Defense of the target by a fixed value, Magic Power, CD, Magical Lifesteal and Movement Speed.

This emblem set has 6 subtalents such as Flow, Mastery, Agility, Observation, Catastrophe and Contract while the 3 main talents are Mystery Shop, Magic worship and Impure Rage.

This emblem is very suitable for using all mages. This emblem is very strong and can use for every mages.

Fighter Emblem

It is highly recommended to use this fighter emblem set for mostly all the fighter because in that emblem, additional physical attacks, physical defense, physical Penetration and HP are added and strongly suitable for fighter heros.

This emblem set has 6 subtalents. They are Bravery, Firmness, Shield, Incasion, Persistence and Swift while the 3 main talents are Unbending Will, Festival of Blood and Disabling Strike.

This emblem is very suitable for various heroes except mages and support. But I saw some tanks and assassins use this emblem to improve their physical attacks.

Support Emblem

Support hero can also increase their stats, such as hybrid regeneration, hybrid penetration, CD, movement speed and HP.

This emblem set also has 6 subtalents. They are Mastery, Vitality, Agility, Gift, Rupture and Recovery while the 3 main talents are Focusing Mark, Avarice and Pull Yourself Together.

It is depends on your need. If you need more support than defend, then you can use support emblem because this emblemm is fully focus on support.

Marksman Emblem

This marksman emblem is actually less popular even in marksman because mostly almost all of marksman use assassin emblem. But, if you want to play a nice critical chance or attack speed, you must use this emblem for only marksman.

For marksman players, they have two choices between assassin emblem and marksman emblem.

This emblem set also has 6 subtalents. They are Bravery, Greed, Fatal, Doom, Agility and Swift while the 3 main talents are Weapon Master, Electro Flash and Weakness Finder.

The Function of Emblem set in Mobile Legends

It is very important for you to focus on the emblems compared to the hero and the equipment because it will be very different that your emblem is in lower state. U will notice when you play with the incomplete emblem set and max emblem set.

The difficulty of playing Mobile Legends as a beginner is the emblem problem. It takes a long tiem to maximize your emblem and if you want it fast you need to spend a lot of battle points.

Each emblem talent branch will be unlocked, once each emblem reaches Lvl. 15. You own 1 talent points at the beginning and get 1 talent point every 5 levels. So you will be able to get all 7 talent points once it reaches Lvl. 45.

Therefore, learn some of the emblems that you can use in common example assassin emblem, support emblem. In addition to stydying emblems, you can also increase knowledge about the equipments in Mobile Legend. So, try your best for the beginners.
