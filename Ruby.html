Ruby

(Fighter/Tank)

Crowd Control/Regen

Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Wolf Slayer

Task Target: Use Spell Vamp, deal Damage to enemies and accumulate 55,000 HP regen.

Ruby had lived in the Black Forest all her youth. She used to love it, but because of the fateful events on that day, she can't bare to think about it. The only thing that matters in her life anymore is finding the man who saved her and mentored her into the skillful killer she is today. Trekking through the Land of Dawn, she gives a swift swing to any that try to push her around again.

Chapter 2 : Anger Flare

Task Target: Use Ult "I'm Offended!" and pull at least 3 enemies. Complete it 2 times.

Once during a dark, howling night, the Wolf King, Pale Tusk led his group of wolves to attack a village. Ruby's old and frail grandfather swore to protect her. But because of his age, he got eaten trying to save her grandmother, who also died. Seeing the bloody murder of her grandparents, she had went through a rite of passage. Her purity and innocence had been stripped and anger fueled her heart. To this day, whenever Ruby sees any wolves, the traumatic memories of that day are triggered and she will become enraged.

Chapter 3 : Hunter & Maiden

Task Target: Complete 2 matches alongside Roger.

A long time ago, the brave hunter, Roger saved a "Little Red Riding Hood" and her family from becoming a pack of wolves' dinner, the Wolf King Pale Tusk's pack. They offered him a place at their house as a token of appreciation and he agreed. Throughout this time, he taught Ruby how to fight, so that if the pack ever returns, she can handle herself. One day, without warning, Roger suddenly left one day without a goodbye. She prayed every night with a heart filled with gratitude and hopes that the hero who helped her once upon a time will return. Her giant scythe glimmers by the girl's side. But one can only pray so much until they lose hope. Ruby decided that she would take matters into her own hands and follow Roger. She had the skills that had been meticulously passed down to her and the glimmer of her scythe's blade.

Chapter 4 : Don't Despise Me

Task Target: Get 20 Kills/Assists with 3 layers of Passive stacked.

Ruby's weak and feeble body often makes opponents underestimate her. It's hard to believe that this little girl has such great strength. But any opponent who has fought against her will be shaken by her power. Ruby acts like a different person in battle, her small frame becomes an advantage in attack, her combat strength increases as she swiftly switches between the attack and defence, and when she gathers enough strength, she will secure victory with a single blow.

Story

It was another peaceful night in a remote village among the Dark Forest before it was interrupted by the vicious howling of the wolves from afar. In a small cabin, a little girl was kneeling on her bed, praying. Next to er stood a disproportionately giant scythe. She was praying for the return of the legendary hunter - Roger. Only he could save the village from being vandalized by the wolves.

Years earlier, Roger saved her family by accident when he was wandering in the Land of Dawn. His bravery took a huge toll on the wolves. At the request from the elderly in the village, Roger finally stayed to train the villagers. One day, he left the village without any notice and no trace to be found.

What happens in the fairy tales can seldom be the same in reaality. On a silent, moonless night, Pale Tusk, the Wolf King, caught the village off guard and sneaked in with his pack. In the end, her grandfather and grandmother died trying to protet Ruby, who in turn made a narrow escape by hiding under the corpses of her grandparents. No one could imagine that from the moment she witnessed her grandparents' death, her character would completely change. Outraged, she got back on her feet, picked up the scythes, rushed into the wolves, and slaughtered almost all of them. Under her seemingly weak appearance now lies an unsetting, irritable soul. Pale Tusk was the only survivor among the wolves. Staring at the skyline, Ruby decided to make a happy ending for all the good people in every story. Pale Tusk was never defeated before but this time it came in the severest way. Ruby gradually regained consciousness after Pale Tusk left. Now, whenever the wolves howl, her eyes become once more filled with rage.
