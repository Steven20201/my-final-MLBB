Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Energy Core

Task Target: Use Noumenon Energy Core and generate Shield for teammates for 30 times.

Lolita was born in a remote elven village, but war brought an end to her peaceful, safe days of the past. During the war, her whole village was destroyed, but just as her own life was in great peril, the great mechanical scientist of Eruditio, Dr. Rooney, came and saved her. To show her gratitude, Lolita applied for Eruditio's city guard, and after obtaining a Noumenon Energy Core, she became a member of the guard. She would always use her Noumenon Energy Core to protect her companions in squad battles.

Chapter 2 : Fire Back

Task Target: Utilizing the Passive of Guardian's Bulwark, fully charge Lolita's Energy Blast by absorbing the maximum damage possible and hit enemy heroes with 8 such blasts.

But Lolita was more that just a defender, she also possessed great attack power, especially with the 'Guardian's Bulwark', a skill which may look like defensive shield but will absorb damage before converting damage into balls and firing them back at opponents, catching them by surprise. The guards of Eruditio all knew her skills very well, with the power of the energy core making her tiny, feeble body capable of unleashing great power. She was even able to wield a giant mechanical hammer with ease.

Chapter 3 : Eruditio Rangers

Task Target: Complete 2 matches with at least 2 Eruditio Rangers in your team.

Lolita recalls that if she hadn't been rescued by Dr. Rooney and taken back to Eruditio back then, then she would probably have died in war a long time ago. In order to repay Eruditio, Lolita has sworn to defend Eruditio's honor to the death. Bruno requested to city scholars for the newest technology crystal from Eruditio - The Noumenon Energy Core, and bestowed it to Lolita. This gave Lolita an even greater feeling of warmth and sense of belonging. As a result, she pays particular attention to Bruno, willing to carry out any order of his.

Chapter 4 : Open Fire

Task Target: Use Noumenon Blast and stun enemies for 40 times.

After obtaining the Noumenon Energy Core, Lolita always hoped to be able to use this precious technology crystal to the fullest, without initially being able to wield its power completly. Under the guidance of Dr. Rooney, Lolita gradually found a ways to use it properly and through stimulation battles arranged by Dr.Rooney, Lolita activated the Noumenon Blast, and was finally able to fully control the Noumenon Energy Core. Afterwards, whenever she was fighting in battle, Lolita would use this newly learnt abilities to defeat her opponents..

Story

Lolita was born in a distant elven village, living a happy, but uneventful life. That is, until war brought an end to those peaceful, safe days. Her whole village was destroyed in the war, her family was completely torn apart. Just as her own life was in grave peril, Dr. Rooney, the master machinist of the City of Scholars, found her when he was passing through and took her back to the Eruditio.

To show her gratitude to the city, Lolita joined the City Guard and swore an oath to defend its honor. Bruno very much admired and understood this young elf maiden's spirit, and so he put in a request with the city scholars for their most advanced tech crystal—the Noumenon Energy Core—and bestowed it to Lolita.

This powerful energy core endows Lolita's tiny frame with the ability to discharge massive Bursts of force, even allowing her to control a giant mechanical hammer with ease.

Some Tutorials for Lolita Gameplay
Best Teammates

Roger: Laning with him is really good because you both have crowd control and damage to kill anyone.

Counters

Kagura: Kagura is a really slippery mage with multiple escape abilities. If you stun her with skill 1, she can get away easily. Her ultimate and teleporting to umbrella can cancel your ultimate, cancelling your most powerful skill. The only way to not get your ultimate interrupted is to pay attention to her skill usage. Make sure she have used both her umbrella teleport and dash, so she have no chance to cancel or escape your ultimate.

Countered by

Cyclops: You can block each and every single attack so he is generally not a huge threat.
