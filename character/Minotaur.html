Minotaur

(Tank/Support)

Crowd Control

Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Maze Trial

Task Target: Use Minoan Fury in rage state and get 6 Kills/Assists.

Living in the Minos Labyrinth, Minotaur used to be the symbol for the strength of the kingdom, that was before the day he was defeated by a youngster from afar. Minotaur then began training himself in the Minos Labyrinth for a long time, hoping to one day find and defeat that youngster. It's also because of such defeat that Minotaur will become enraged, and this rage has become a part of his ability. When enraged, Minotaur is quite terrifying.

Chapter 2 : Control Rage

Task Target: Take more than 40,000 Damage and win the match. Complete it 3 times.

Time has taught Minotaur some senses, he has learned how to control his own rage to boost his own power to greater extend. As a result, Minotaur has learned endurance, not just in psychological sense, but also as a way to effectively use his rage, allowing him to use this strength in crucial moments. After enduring a great amount of damage, Minotaur will use rage at the crucial moment to unleash his vengeance onto the opponent in one burst.

Chapter 3 : Revenge

Task Target: Complete 30 times of Revenge (Kills/Assists) to enemies who have slayed you.

As he is easily enraged, it means that he does now easily forgive those who have wronged him. His personality is extremely persistent and resolute. When facing with opponents who have hurt or defeated him in the past, Minotaur just has one thought: if he could meet them again, if he has another chance for a rematch, he won't ever want to miss the chance. He couldn't allow himself to be defeated a second time by the same opponent or in the same place.

Chapter 4 : Guard Twilight Orb

Task Target: Get 3 double win streaks using Minotaur.

Legend has it that there is an ancient book of enlightenment in Minos Labyrinth, many great warriors have come there in hopes of taking this book. The heroes summoned by Minotaur are all gathered by the entrance to the Emerald Road, determined to help the Prince of Minos to guard their book of enlightenment. Faced with many skilled warriors, Minotaur won't allow anyone to destroy the Minos Labyrinth an take the book of enlightenment.

Story

In older times, the Minoans founded a mysterious and prosperous state named Minoa in Agelta Drylands.

The once-bustling Emerald Road connected Minoa with other western cities, creating a network of trade routes, while Minoa stood tall among all others with its advanced civilization and martial traditions.

Prince Minotaur of Minoa was the most esteemed warrior back then. Passionate about fighting and often fueled by his rage, Minotaur would demonstrate terrifyingbursts of strength in battle. Few were those who could even hope to last more than a few rounds against him.

The people grew to worship Minotaur, yet his father was deeply concerned about him. While reaping great success on the battlefield, Minotaur's savagery and impulsive behavior was unbecoming of the successor of the state.

To forge Minotaur into a suitable heir to the throne, the old king arranged for a special trial - one in which the prince must defeat a mysterious figure from foreign lands to qualify for the throne of Minoa.

Minotaur accepted this unarmed duel without hesitation. He stepped onto the arena with his head held high and attempted to crush his opponent with brute force as he'd always done. However, his opponent this time was much tougher, and Minotaur had soon exhausted himself, losing his advantage.

In the end, the mysterious figure defeated the "invincible" Minotaur with measured counterattacks. The crowd cheered for the strong and graceful victor, yet Minotaur was overwhelmed by rage and shame. And when the mysterious figure stretched out his hand to help Minotaur get back to his feet, Minotaur grabbed the unsuspecting hand and pinned the mysterious figure to the ground, before throwing a series of vicious punches at him.

As his fists were covered in blood and the mysterious figure stopped struggling, Minotaur tore off the latter's mask amidst the boos of the crowd - and that was when his whole body stiffened. The severenly injured figure on the ground was actually his father!

The old king grasped Minotaur's hand with his last strength and uttered his final words of instruction to his son, "You must learn to shoulder the responsibility as a king and continue the glory of Minoa..."

Consumed by shame and regret, Minotaur headed to the Minoan Labyrinth - the burial ground of the Minoan ancestors - and began his training and repentance.

Over timee, Minotaur gradually gained control over his rage and become able to tap into his raging powers at will.

As Minotaur thrust himself into this arduous training, an unexpected catastrophe struck. The entire Miona was flattened by the explosion of the Twilight Orb, and only MMinotaur was left unharmed under the protection of the Labyrinth. Minotaur rushed toward the state amidst the tremors, wanting to find out what had happened, when a broken piece from the Twilight Orb plummeted into the Labyrinth and sealed it whole. Minotaur, who was half a step out of the Labyrinth, was sealed altogether and put into a deep slumber.

The catastrophe erased all traces of the Minoan civilization, leaving only the Minoan Labyrinth half-hidden in the sand. After countless days and nights, the broken piece from the Twilight Orb turned into Lunox and left.

THe seal was then lifted. Time in the Labyrinth began to flow again, while Minotaur slowly opened his eyes..

Aldous, the guardian of the Labyrinth, told Minotaur his state had long turned into dust, and he and his memories were all that remained. Minotaur, however, would believe none of those.

With his father's final entrustment still fresh in his mind, the prince vowed to himself that he must find his list kin and rebuild the Minoan civilization.

In the boundless sands, a set of hood-prints extended into the horizon...

Rage Incarnate

Death Immune
Passive

Minotaur gains Rage each time he hits his target with a skill or Basic Attack, When at max Rage, Minotaur can cast Enhanced Minoan Fury to enter the Enraged state, gaining 20 (+3*Hero Level) extra Physical Attack and 10 (+4*Hero Level) Physical and Magic Defense (scales with level). He'll also reset his skill cooldowns and gain enhanced skill effects. After leaving the Enraged state, Minotaur will be unable to gain Rage or cast Minoan Fury for a short while.
