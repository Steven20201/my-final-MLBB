Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Inspiring Panda

Task Target: Use Akai and like your allies, a total of 10 times.

In a remote Eastern village, there lived a Panda named Akai. Since his youth, Akai was determined to become a mighty warrior. While everyone though that he was joking, Akai shocked them all, with his persistence. In order to gain everyone's respect, he searched for skilled opponents day and night, giving sincere compliments to stronger opponents, hoping to learn more powerful battle techniques from them. As a result, he did get lots of support and encouragement, from everyone he battled.

Chapter 2 : Practice Hard

Task Target: Use Akai and partake in matches for three consecutive days.

Even after the constant discouragement showered on him, the chubby Akai never gave up, and under the guidance of an experienced Frog Hermit, he became a skilled warrior. He trained hard, to protect the Eastern village and also constantly looked for worthy opponents, in order to hone his skills. It was this persistence that the Frog Hermit valued greatly. He would arrange fights with various opponents each day, to learn new battle techniques and gain more experience. And in doing so, his martial arts ability, improved day after day.

Chapter 3 : Mentor's Instruction

Task Target: Use Blender and Thousand Pounder to accumulate a total of 30 stun effects on enemies.

At first, Akai was worried that his cumbersome body would become a burden, during quick battles. Subsequently, this thought affected Akai's confidence. But under the guidance of the Frog Hermit, Akai learned advanced techniques and abilities to leverage his body to his advantage. During the course of every combat, Akai would use his comparatively huge size to deliver punishing blows and crush his opponents, under his weight.

Chapter 4 : Unrivalled

Task Target: Use Hurricane Dance and accumulate 500 collides on enemy heroes

Overtime, his body didn't limit Akai's agility and combat speed at all and henceforth, his confidence swiftly returned. Through training and battles, Akai became proud of his progress towards becoming a skilled warrior with greatly improved combat techniques. On the move, Akai adapted his battle techniques into a bamboo rod, quickly learning to spin it like a hurricane. He soon mastered this style of combat, making it his signature move.

Story

A clumsy panda from a far away Eastern village dreamt every night of becoming a mighty warrior. Akai was fat and clumsy even for panda standards and most people said that all he's good for was rolling round.

However, this never fazed Akai, as he trained daily, hoping to one day achieve his dreams of becoming a respected warrior. His friends mocked him, his parents discouraged him but Akai persisted. Soon one day, a mysterious old monk noticed Akai's persistence and took him under his guidance. Akai flourished under the training of the monk, mastering the arts of the flail.

He is now ready to get onto the world stage and prove that he is a worthy member of the martial arts world!
