Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : To be a Killer

Task Target: Accumulate 15 Kills of enemies.

Because of the misfortunes in the family, Lesley grew up to become a merciless killer. Her father died in an attempt to protect Harley's family. As the assailants approached the Vance household, Lesley leapt out from the corner, took her father's black long rifle and finished off her late father's mission. The tears rolling down her cheeks didn't stop her from pulling the trigger, each shot taking down one enemy. She would not stop shooting until all her enemies fell down.

Chapter 2 :Single Shot

Task Target: Deal 3 times of Critical Damage above 1800.

To protect those she cares about from being hurt again, Lesley keeps improving her marksmanship and fighting skill. She gives her all in every battle, hoping to avoid even the slightest mistake as she never wants to say goodbye to one of her loves one again. After obtaining the ultimate armor, Berserker's Fury, she realized that her damage was greatly increased and that she often can kill her target with a single shot.

Chapter 3 : Protection

Task Target: Fight alongside Harley for 2 times.

Her little brother Harley, mischiefs are the last bit of comfort for her, and his safety is her primary concern. She doesn't want him to suffer any harm, so when she heard that Harley went through a portal to the dangerous Land of Dawn, she took her long black rifle and followed suit. After crossing through hardships and perils to find Harley, she discovered that the once naughty young boy was undergoing changes due to the experiences with his close friends and his adventures. She decided to let him stay there for now to grow stronger, while watching over him from the shadows.

Chapter 4 : Ace Sniper

Task Target: Use Ultimate Snipe and accumulate 15 Kills of enemies.

Lesley learned Ultimate Snipe, after which she earned the most terrifying title - Ace Sniper. From that day onwards, any evil creatures that dared to approach her or Harley were killed from afar by a single shot of her sniper rifle. Over time, tales of this magical youth and the black gun behind him spread fear over the forces of evil.

Story

Lesley grew up in the Vance household, where her father presided over the noble family's security. During an unexpected attack by a rival family, Lesley's father was killed. As the assailants approached the Vance household, Lesley took up her father's black long rifle and fulfilled her late father's mission. Tears rolled down Lesley's cheeks as she fired shot after shot, surprisingly, each one taking down an assassin. The final tear came as the final shot took down the last of the rival family's men.

Lesley was adopted by the Vance family, with the patriarch of the noble house even putting their sole heir, Harley under her tutelage. Harley, was a natural troublemaker, who used his talents in magic to unintentionally cause trouble for the Vance household. Only Lesley could keep Harley under control. Harley's troublemaking streak was the last part of warmth within Lesley's conscience.

After hearing about how Harley had travelled to the dangerous Land of Dawn after defeating the Boss of Dark Magic, Lesley followed him, along with her trusty heirloom rifle. After she found Harley through a long arduous journey, she discovered that the once immature young boy [has] changed through the numerous life experiences he had gone through. She decided it would be best to have her adopted little brother train, alone, while she would silently protect him from within the shadows. From that day on, all the monsters that were inflicted with Harley's magic would also be struck with a black bullet, that would impeccably pierce through their heart. The stories of the magical youngster who was followed by a mysterious black sniper, soon spread across the Land of Dawn, like wild fire.

Lethal Shot

BUFF
Passive

Lesley restores 5 energy each time she hits an enemy with her Basic Attack. If she doesn't take damage for 5s, her next Basic Attack gains extra attack range and 50% Crit Chance and deals 1.2 times damage. The enhanced Basic Attack also restores twice the amount of energy. (Lethal Shot) deals 20% extra damage to minions. The cooldown of (Lethal Shot) is reset each time Lesley casts a skill. Each point of fixed Physical Penetration Lesley acquires will be converted into 1% Crit Chance (percentage Physical Penetration isn't affected).

Master of Camouflage

BUFF
Conceal
CD: 5.0 Energy Cost: 30

Lesley enters the Camouflage state and gains double energy restoration, 40% extra Movement Speed, and 75 Physical Attack for up to 3s.

Dealing or taking damage will end this state. Enemies can detect Lesley's camouflage through distortions of their surroundings.

Best Teammates

Akai: Lesley’s damage output is high, but she lacks spacing abilities, and she is often rendered vulnerable to attack, especially when using her ultimate. She depends on teammates who can provide her with adequate protection, such as Lolita, with her shield, and Akai, with his knockback skill.

Counters

Saber: Lesley is easily targeted by assassins, and Lolita’s ultimate can grant her a shield, while slowing and stunning Lesley.

Countered by

Odette: When Odette activates her ultimate skill, she becomes the perfect target for Lesley’s ultimate.
