Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Welcome to Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Welcome to Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle ARENA (MOBA) developed and published by Moonton. Released in 11 July 2016, the game has become popular in SouthEast Asia and was among the games chosen for the first battle event E-sports competition at 2019 SouthEast Asia Games in the Philippines.

In July 2018, Tencent on behalf of Riot Games won a lawsuit in Shanghai against Moonton CEO Watson Xu Zhenhua for Mobile Legends' plagiarism of League of Legends and was awarded a settlement of $2.9 million.

After Moonton's staff of 20 finished developing its first game called Magic Rush: Heroes, released in 2015 to commercial success, they proceeded with developing the company's next project, a mobile multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game later titled Mobile Legends.

What is New? (Patch Notes 1.6.58 (02/21/2022))

Mobile Legends Patch 1.6.58 Update: Hero adjustments

New Hero

[New Hero: Cursed Neddle - Melissa]

Hero Feature: A rebellious girl that runs a tailor shop and solves problems for people. This new Marksman hero also has unique skill description.

[Passive: Doll Buster]

Mellisa deals 150% damage to Minions and Summoned Units.

[Skill 1: Falling!]

Mellisa slides forward and gains exta Attack Speed for 3s.She also commands[Muddles], to move a distance in the same direction and [Cuddles] to move the field to her destination when the two are on the field.

[Skill 2: Eye on You!]

Mellisa throws out [Muddles], inflicting damage and slow while linking nearby enemy heroes up to 6s. When there are linked enemies, Melissa will command [Giggles] to attack [Muddles] on her each Basic Attack, dealing the same amount of damage to the linked enemies. Linked enemies can break the link by moving out of the range.

[Ultimate: Go Away!]

Mellisa commands [Cuddles] to release the Field of protection, dealing damage while blocking enemies from entering the field for 4s. The field will also interrupt the enemy's movement skills. The field will disappear early if Melissa leaves the field.

Hero Adjustments

Heroes with major adjustments in the patch include: Diggie, Silvanna, Karrie, Gloo, Yin and Valentina.
