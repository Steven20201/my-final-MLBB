Johnson

(Tank)

Support/Crowd Control

Mastery Code

Chapter 1 :Iron Armour

Task Target: Gain the most Physical Defense when a match end. Complete it 2 times.

Johnson was a famous quarterback when he was younger, but his life changed drastically after a near-fatal car crash. His rapidly declining life sign was saved and revitalized in the city of Eruditio. Dr. Rooney discovered that Johnson's body was an extremely rare specimen that could be integrated with machinery. After Dr. Rooney's treatment, Johnson was miraculously resuscitated. He is now built like a brick house and the perfect integration between his body and machinery makes him the strongest defensive unit. He can bear a strong attack and remains unaffected, which gives Johnson a lot of confidence.

Chapter 2 : Fast & Furious/h1>

Task Target: Use Rapid Touchdown and take The Reliables for a ride. Complete it 4 times.

Johnson has always believed in miracles, as an athlete or owner of a mechanical body, he believes that he has been blessed by miracles. He doesn't think less of himself because of his mechanical body and enjoys how it is now. Regarding the ability to transform into a sports car, he is thrilled and would drag all his friends into the car to experience the excitement of his fast and furious driving.

Chapter 3 : Unbreakable Shield

Task Target: Win a match with more than 25% Damage taken (of the team). Complete 3 such matches.

After such misfortune, Johnson not only stubbornly clung onto life but also became a fantastical new type of human that could transform into a sports car. Johnson is extremely grateful to Dr Rooney and wants to help Eruditio; Johnson and the city guards have become the impenetrable barrier of Eruditio. Whenever the city is attacked and damaged by outsiders, Johnson always steps up at the first opportunity. He not only protects the city but also protects the city guards. He is willing to fight on the front line and endure all the damage that the enemy deals.

Chapter 4 : Rapid Dash

Task Target: Hit an enemy hero with Rapid Touchdown. Complete it 50 times.

Johnson is built like a brick house; whenever he rushes by like a truck, his power and velocity are enough to get you pumped up. Johnson has a hard body and can deal decent damage. He will take one ally and charge and electrifying power are his signature counterattack moves; the sound of Johnson starting up his sports car is like the battle horn for counterattack.

Story

Johnson, with a hardy constitution as solid as a rock, ran through the dome stadium like a heavy truck, the cheers of girls bursting from everywhere. He led his team to lift the champion's trophy and achieved the highpoint of his life as a superstar quarterback with millions of fans. However, fortune is always fickle. A little girl suddenly rushed into the road while Johnson was driving his sports car. To avoid hitting the little girl he swerved and severely crashed his car. A massive hemorrhage was going to take his life quickly.

At that moment, a miracle occurred. The wreckage of the sports car started to combine with Johnson's body by a mysterious force. Because of this, he not only survived the crash but also became a magial new human who could transform into a sports car.
