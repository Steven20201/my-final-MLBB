Johnson

(Tank)

Support/Crowd Control

Mastery Code

Chapter 1 : Mischievous Witch

Task Target: Get the most Assists of both sides when the match ends. Complete it 1 time.

Johnson is a special Leonin, native to the Enchanted Forest. Joyful and outgoing since youth, She loves adventures and can unintentionally be mischievous at times, often causing trouble. But she has a great dream of one day becoming a powerful Leonin Mage. Since she first started fighting, Johnson didn't have the heart to land a fatal blow on opponents and often found herself supporting others, Rather than knocking them out.

Chapter 2 : Discovery Trip

Task Target: Use Rage Incarnate and deal Damage twice to the same enemy. Complete it 80 times.

She once met the Moonlight Archer, Miya on the road, who taught her to control her magical power. As a result, she was extremely grateful to Miya. They thus decided to travel together, with the same goal, of finding a prosperous king, who could lead their clans to success. During their journey, they ran into many different opponents and Johnson would always assist Miya, Finishing enemies off with her Boomerang at the crucial moment. After such aids, Johnson earned praises from Miya, which made her determined to continue honing, Both her Magic and Boomerang skill.

Chapter 3 : Magic Tricks

Task Target: Get 10 Kills/Assists of enemies you have transformed

Johnson's powers increased much efficiency, After training for a long time. Miya became confident to let Johnson face powerful opponents on her own. As a prankster, Johnson still liked to play tricks on people during battle, especially by toying with her opponent with the Molina Smooch. What makes her happiest is to see those who underestimated and mocked her, Turn into a cute little rabbit. This became one of her main ways to harass her opponents, before defeating them.

Chapter 4 : Outraged Leonin

Task Target: Use Ult and accumulate 25 Stuns to enemies

Johnson may have been mischievious and cutesy, but that was not how Miya saw her. A serious Johnson could be quite terrifying, as Miya vaguely remembered an outraged Johnson almost destroying half of the Enchanted forest. It would be best not to anger her in battle or else she can most probably, use her most powerful ability 'Minoan Fury' to let opponent, know the meaning of shock and awe.
